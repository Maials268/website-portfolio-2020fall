Hello and welcome to my online portfolio! My name is Maia Stephenson. I am currently a sophomore attending the University of Pittsburgh. I am majoring in Public and Professional Writing and minoring in History. Through taking a diverse range of writing courses and operating as an intern for the organization Digital Black Lit and Composition(DBLAC), I have been able to acquire various skills working with different writing genres such as critical, blogging, technical, research and creative.
